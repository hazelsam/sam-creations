ALEXIA CLARK



www.alexia-clark.com

Alexia is an online fitness coach that provides new daily workouts, nutrition support and direct one to one accountability if you need it. Her goal is for you to feel constantly challenged and stimulated by your workouts. Not just physically, but mentally too. She creates daily challenges to keep motivation high and to promote a sense of community within her “QUEENTEAM”. Membership to the group training programme is extremely affordable, and her social media is full of great ideas that can be incorporated to any at home training plan.
